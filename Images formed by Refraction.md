## Equation: $$\frac{n_a}{s} + \frac{n_b}{s'} = \frac{n_b-n_a}{R}$$
^ Tells you the distances of images when the spherical surface has refraction too
Derivation is from [[Law of Refraction]]

### Magnification : $m = \frac{y}{y'} = -\frac{n_aS'}{n_bS}$
